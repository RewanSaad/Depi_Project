Cihha Berry Budding is a refreshing, light, and healthy fusion recipe combining elements from both traditional and modern kitchens...

Ingredients

  • Cihha berries (about 100 grams)
  • 1 ripe banana (peeled)
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • Honey or sugar (to taste)
  • 1 cup almond milk (or any milk of your choice)
  • Cinnamon (optional, a small pinch)
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • A pinch of salt (optional)
  • Ground ginger
  • 1/4 cup grated cucumber
  • 1 tbsp fresh mint (chopped)
  • 1/4 tsp cumin powder (optional)

Step by step method

Step 1

Add Cihha berries, the banana, yogurt, honey (or sugar), and almond milk to a blender,Blend until smooth and creamy.

Step 2

If you like a warm flavor, add a pinch of cinnamon and blend again.

Step 3

Let the berries marinate in the mixture for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

Tips & variations

  • ✓ Berry choice: If Cihha berries are unavailable, you can substitute with cranberries or pomegranate seeds for a similar tartness.
  • ✓ Enhancing flavor: Add a few drops of rose water or a pinch of cardamom in the shake or raita for a more exotic flavor profile.
  • ✓ Perfect marination: For a richer flavor, allow the berries to marinate for a longer time, even up to 2-3 hours if possible.
