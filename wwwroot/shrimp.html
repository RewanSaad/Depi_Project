These Shrimp Tacos are a flavor-packed, vibrant, and easy-to-make dish. Juicy, marinated shrimp are pan-seared and tucked into soft tortillas with a crisp slaw and creamy, spicy shake sauce. Perfect for summer nights, family dinners, or casual get-togethers — they deliver the perfect mix of freshness, spice, and creaminess in every bite.

Ingredients

  • 300g shrimp (peeled & deveined)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 cup shredded red cabbage
  • 2 tbsp sour cream or Greek yogurt
  • Small flour or corn tortillas
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • A pinch of salt (optional)
  • Ground ginger
  • 1/4 cup grated cucumber
  • 1 tbsp fresh mint (chopped)
  • 1/4 tsp cumin powder (optional)

Step by step method

Step 1

In a bowl, toss the shrimp with olive oil, lemon/lime juice, garlic powder, paprika, chili powder, cumin, salt, and pepper.
Let it marinate for 20 minutes.

Step 2

In a separate bowl, combine shredded cabbage, carrots, cilantro, lime juice, olive oil, salt, and pepper.
Mix well and refrigerate until serving.

Step 3

In a small bowl, whisk together mayonnaise, sour cream, lime juice, hot sauce, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
Adjust seasoning as desired and chill it.

Step 4

Heat a pan over medium-high heat.
Cook the shrimp for 2-3 minutes per side until pink and cooked through.

Step 5

Warm the tortillas.
Add a layer of slaw, top with shrimp, drizzle with shake sauce, and garnish with avocado slices and fresh herbs.
Serve with lime wedges on the side.

Tips & variations

  • ✓ Don’t overcook the shrimp — they get rubbery fast. 2-3 minutes per side is perfect.
  • ✓ You can swap the slaw for diced mango salsa or avocado-tomato salad for a twist.
  • ✓ Warm your tortillas before serving to make them soft and pliable.
